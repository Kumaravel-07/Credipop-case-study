About Credipop

At CrediPop, our mission is simple: to stop the spread of fake news and manipulated images. We believe that everyone deserves access to accurate, truthful information. By offering innovative solutions for verifying sources and visual content, we help create a more informed and responsible digital community. How It Works. That’s where CrediPop comes in. Our app is built to empower individuals, communities, and organizations to become more informed and responsible digital citizens. By offering an easy-to-use solution that verifies the authenticity of news stories and visual content, CrediPop equips users with the knowledge and tools needed to navigate today’s fast-paced media landscape.

Our Mission

At CrediPop, we are driven by one core mission: to stop the spread of misinformation, whether it be fake news, misleading visuals, or distorted facts. Our goal is to restore trust in the information ecosystem by equipping people with the ability to verify content before engaging with or sharing it

How Credipop Works

Using CrediPop is simple. Whether you’re on your phone or desktop

1. Upload or Enter Content: Whether it’s a headline, article, or image, simply enter the URL or upload the content you want to verify.

2. Get Instant Feedback: Within seconds, our system analyzes the content, checking it against multiple databases, metadata, and visual patterns to provide you with a detailed report.

2

3. Take Action: After reviewing the report, you’ll have the tools to make an informed decision about whether to trust, share, or report the content. You can also easily share this verified information with your networks.
